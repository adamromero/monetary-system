Step 1: Government Creates US Treasury Bonds
When the U.S. Government uses money for deficit spending, be it military, infrastructure, etc., it goes to the U.S. Treasury where it issues treasury bonds. These bonds are what make up the nation's national debt since bonds are IOU's (I own you) and therefore must eventually be paid back with interest. The treasury holds bond auctions, big banks buy these bonds and make a profit off the interest.
govt building -> us treasury -> us treasury bonds -> banks
Step 2: Banks trade Bonds to create US Currency
Through Open Market Operations, the treasure bonds are sold to the Federal Reserve at a profit for the banks. To pay for the bonds The Federal Reserve creates checks on an account with no bank deposit, creating currency the Fed gives currency to the banks, which they use to buy more bonds us treasury and the fed swap bonds and currency with banks as middle men this process raises the national debt
